Privacy Policy

YouTube Easy Unsubscribe Extension

Last updated: 29-Dec-2024

1. Data Collection and Usage

YouTube Easy Unsubscribe is designed with user privacy in mind. We are committed to being transparent about our data handling practices:

  • Website Content Access: The extension reads YouTube channel names and subscription status from the YouTube website solely to provide the unsubscribe functionality.
  • User Activity: We monitor user interactions (clicks) specifically on YouTube's subscription page to handle the unsubscribe actions and checkbox interactions.

2. Data Storage

All data processing occurs locally within your browser:

  • Temporary storage is used only to maintain the list of selected channels during the unsubscribe process
  • No data is stored permanently
  • All temporary data is automatically cleared after completing the unsubscribe actions

3. Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Share any data with third parties
  • Transfer any information outside of your local browser
  • Use analytics or tracking tools

4. Permissions Usage

The extension requires certain permissions to function:

  • activeTab: To interact with YouTube subscription pages
  • host permission (youtube.com): To modify YouTube's interface for unsubscribe functionality
  • storage: For temporary storage of selected channels during the unsubscribe process
  • scripting: To add checkboxes and handle unsubscribe actions
  • tabs: To manage the batch unsubscribe process

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.
